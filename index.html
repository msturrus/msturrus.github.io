About

I am a clever full-stack developer who enjoys finding unorthodox and interesting ways to tackle problems. In all my projects, at all times, I keep the user's convenience and entertainment at the forefront of my mind.

I like node.js, React, Three.js, Ruby, Rails, HTML5/CSS3, various databases and ORMS, and more, but what i really like is learning new things. I am available for work in the Chicago area.
